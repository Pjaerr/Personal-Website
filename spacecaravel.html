Space Caravel

Go Back

Space Caravel is a 2D wave based game wherein all of the levels are randomly generated and you must survive all of the waves of enemies to complete the level. Whilst doing this, you must upgrade your ship to keep up with the varying difficulty.

This game was made for #30DayDev, where the goal is to complete a game within 30 days of work.

Made with Unity and C#.

Timespan: Ongoing.
